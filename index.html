Grown, Raised, and Made in Alabama

Do you love fresh, locally-grown produce? Are you looking for high-quality, handmade products from Alabama farmers and small business owners? Look no further than Hueytown Harvest, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization and certified farmers market by the Alabama Farmers Market Authority.

At Hueytown Harvest, everything is grown, raised, and made in Alabama. The farmers who grow the produce bring it fresh to the market—no franchises and no reselling allowed. By supporting Hueytown Harvest, you are directly supporting Alabama farmers and small business owners who are committed to providing you with unique, high-quality products. The market also accepts the SFMNP Benefits Card, making it easy for seniors to access fresh, locally-grown produce.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to support local farmers while enjoying delicious, premium goods. Check out Hueytown Harvest today!
